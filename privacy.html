Soundvibe - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

1. Overview

Soundvibe ("the App," "we," "us") is a desktop application that runs locally on your computer and helps you manage, schedule, and publish your own social media content to platforms including TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, where it is stored, and how you can delete it or revoke access.

2. Where Your Data Lives

Soundvibe is a local-first application. We do not operate cloud servers, and we do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server owned or operated by Soundvibe. Your media files, post history, captions, scheduling data, and all platform access tokens are stored only in a local database and configuration files on your own computer.

3. TikTok Integration

When you choose to connect your TikTok account, Soundvibe uses TikTok's official Login Kit and Content Posting API. This integration is entirely optional and is only activated if you explicitly connect TikTok from the Settings page.

Permission scopes we request:

ScopeWhy we request it
user.info.basicTo display your TikTok account so you can confirm you are publishing to the correct profile (we receive your TikTok open ID, display name, and avatar).
video.upload / video.publishTo upload and post the videos you create and select within Soundvibe to your own TikTok account.

TikTok data we access, and how we handle it:

  • Basic profile information (open ID, display name, avatar) — used only to show you which account is connected. Stored locally on your device.
  • OAuth access & refresh tokens — stored locally and used solely to publish content on your behalf when you request it.
  • The videos and captions you choose to publish — sent directly from your device to TikTok's API at the moment you publish.

We do not read, collect, or store your TikTok followers, your videos already on TikTok, your private messages, your analytics, or any other TikTok user's data. We never sell or share TikTok data with any third party. Soundvibe's use of information received from TikTok APIs adheres to the TikTok Developer Terms of Service, and our use and transfer of that information complies with TikTok's requirements.

How to revoke TikTok access: You can disconnect TikTok at any time from the Settings page inside Soundvibe, which deletes the stored tokens from your device. You may also revoke Soundvibe's access directly in your TikTok account at Settings and privacy → Security and permissions → Manage app permissions.

4. Other Information We Access

Soundvibe accesses the following information solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Social media account tokens: When you connect a platform (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram), the App stores OAuth access tokens locally to publish content on your behalf.
  • Media files: Videos and images you upload are stored locally on your device for publishing.
  • Post metadata: Captions, hashtags, scheduling times, and publish status are stored in a local database.

5. Third-Party Services

Soundvibe communicates with the following third-party services only when you use the related feature:

  • TikTok API: To authenticate your account and publish videos. Subject to TikTok's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube API: To authenticate your channel and upload videos. Subject to YouTube's Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Instagram API: To authenticate your account and publish content. Subject to Instagram's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic API: To generate AI-powered captions and hashtags. Media frames may be sent to Anthropic's API for analysis. Subject to Anthropic's policies.

6. Data Sharing

Soundvibe does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Data is only sent to the social media platforms you explicitly choose to publish to and to the AI service for caption generation, in each case at your direction.

7. Data Security

API keys and authentication tokens are stored in local configuration files on your device. Soundvibe uses OAuth 2.0 for platform authentication, meaning your social media passwords are never seen or stored by the App.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

Because all data is stored locally, you control it entirely. You can delete it at any time by disconnecting a platform in Settings (which removes that platform's stored tokens), deleting media from within the App, removing the App's data directory, or uninstalling Soundvibe. We retain no copy of your data because we never receive one.

9. Children's Privacy

Soundvibe is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
