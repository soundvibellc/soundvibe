Soundvibe - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

1. Overview

Soundvibe ("the App") is a desktop application that runs locally on your computer. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Soundvibe does not operate cloud servers and does not transmit your personal data to any server owned or operated by Soundvibe. Your media files, post history, captions, and scheduling data remain on your computer in a local database.

3. Information We Collect

Soundvibe accesses the following information solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Social media account tokens: When you connect a platform (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram), the App stores OAuth access tokens locally to publish content on your behalf.
  • Media files: Videos and images you upload are stored locally on your device for publishing.
  • Post metadata: Captions, hashtags, scheduling times, and publish status are stored in a local database.

4. Third-Party Services

Soundvibe communicates with the following third-party services:

  • TikTok API: To authenticate your account and publish videos. Subject to TikTok's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube API: To authenticate your channel and upload videos. Subject to YouTube's Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic API: To generate AI-powered captions and hashtags. Media frames may be sent to Anthropic's API for analysis. Subject to Anthropic's policies.

5. Data Sharing

Soundvibe does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Data is only sent to the social media platforms you explicitly choose to publish to and to the AI service for caption generation.

6. Data Security

API keys and authentication tokens are stored in encrypted local configuration files on your device. Soundvibe uses OAuth 2.0 for platform authentication, meaning your social media passwords are never stored by the App.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by removing the App's data directory or uninstalling the application. You can also disconnect any social media platform from the Settings page, which removes stored tokens.

8. Children's Privacy

Soundvibe is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our GitHub repository.
